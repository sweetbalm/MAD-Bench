📊 Database Official Recommended Configurations

Best practices & production-ready presets based on vendor guidelines

PostgreSQL ACID · Advanced
Official v16+ / v17 LTS
⚙️ Key Parameters (postgresql.conf)
  • max_connections200 – 500 (application‑specific)
  • shared_buffers25% of total RAM (e.g., 8GB for 32GB system)
  • effective_cache_size50% – 75% of total RAM
  • work_mem4MB – 16MB (adjust based on sorting/hashing)
  • maintenance_work_mem512MB – 2GB (for vacuum, create index)
  • wal_buffers16MB – 64MB (auto-tuned or manual)
  • checkpoint_completion_target0.9 (smooth I/O)
  • max_wal_size2GB – 8GB (depending on write workload)
🖥️ Hardware / OS Recommendations
  • CPUMinimum 4 cores, recommended 8+ cores for production
  • RAM8GB – 128GB+ (scale with dataset & workload)
  • StorageNVMe SSD or enterprise-grade SSD; RAID 10 for high availability
  • FilesystemXFS or ext4 with noatime, barrier=0 for performance
💡 Official tuning: Use `pg_tune` calculator or PGTune. Enable huge pages for large shared_buffers. Monitor with pg_stat_statements.

✨ Based on official vendor documentation & field-tested configurations — always adapt values to your workload, dataset size, and SLA requirements.
